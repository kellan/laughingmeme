Feb 26, 2004 • Kellan

Quinn posted a description of her dream conference, the o’reilly technology toward improvement conference with tracks like:

  • women and minorities
  • democratizing education
  • emerging markets
  • personal sovereignty
  • information dissemination

Sounds like it would be a good collaboration between Ruckus, and O’Reilly. ### Geek Organizer

Related news MoveOn is launching a “Geek Organizer” online job fair (for a org which everyone says is so savvy, they make it incredibly difficult to link to their news) > Within our team, we’ve identified a crucial role we call (affectionately) the Geek Organizer. No, that’s not an organizer of geeks. It’s a person who combines the leadership and communication skills of a political organizer with the technical skills of a computer nerd. How rare are these people? We don’t know. But we know they exist, because we’ve built our organization around them.

I don’t know, seems every third person I know fits that description. ### CATS
