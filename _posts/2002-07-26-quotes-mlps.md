[ufobreakfast on pundits](http://www.ufobreakfast.com/archive/00000179.htm) – *“The whole point of pundity is reduce discourse to an extension of raw physical or economic force: raw repetition and numbers win, period. The quality of argumentative content is of superficial concern. The “fact-checking” of any numerical minority is powerless in this scenario–which is why only a large, well-funded and arrogant majority coalition would risk poisoning the infrastructure of their entire culture in this way….We are not going to be saved by hyperlinks.”* \[[via moreLikeThis](http://www.whump.com/moreLikeThis/link/02982)\]

[cr8dle2grave ](http://www.kuro5hin.org/comments/2002/6/25/173/40630/85#85)*“My personal politics are a little right of center on issues of economics and foreign policy, but I’ve got to say AIM are a bunch chest thumping hillbillies.”* **LOL**. Responding to claims that AIM is FAIR for the right. The thread starts with a [nice piece on the Accuracy In the Media](http://www.kuro5hin.org/story/2002/6/25/173/40630) foundation.

[from the same thread](http://www.prospect.org/print/V13/8/nunberg-g.html) – *Bernard Goldberg may not be wrong about the presence of bias in the media — he’s just wrong that it’s “liberal.”*

[TomPaine is asking](http://www.tompaine.com/op_ads/opad.cfm/ID/6044) – *“Can Liberals save Capitalism (Again)?”* But who would want to? Still got love the slogan *“Laissez-Faire Itself Is The Ultimate Corporate Fraud”*

[the Reg, at its usual poignant best](http://www.theregister.co.uk/content/6/26357.html) – *“Anyone who publishes original content will be able to fend off the FBI with a simple, “d00d, I thought they were archiving my blogs, honest.”*

And finally  
[moreLikeThis](http://www.whump.com/moreLikeThis/link/02985) – *Mars looks better every freaking day*
