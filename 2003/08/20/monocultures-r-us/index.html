Monocultures R Us

Aug 20, 2003 • Kellan

I have to say waking up to a Pine inbox full of helpful automated notes suggesting I fix my Outlook install is fucking annoying. This SoBig Windows virus going around is gleefully sticking any address it finds in the infected computers addressbook or cached html files into not only the To: field but also the From: field. I have this dream of class action lawsuit with Microsoft and Monsanto as a dual defendants accussed of being piss poor engineer who have managed to perpetuate a monoculture of unsafe, untested, poorly designed products upon the world.
