Nutch – an "open source" search engine

Aug 14, 2003 • Kellan

You probably saw the Slashdot story on Nutch, and if, like me, you’re bored and annoyed by Slashdot ignored it.

A few pieces of info that make Nutch more interesting:

  • Doug Cutting, one of the leading, and most innovative search engine hackers, the man behind both VTwin and Lucene is the main hacker on Nutch
  • Almost a year of active development has already gone into this
  • Tim O’Reilly, and Mitch Kapor (and Doug Cutting) are on the board.

Making ones biases public is a one of the key ideas behind the IMC open publishing movement, so I’m thrilled by the idea of an open source search engine. But what is with the crappy name?

I might have to add an ngo/npo technology category.
