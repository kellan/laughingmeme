Harpers, the Web as it was Meant to Be

Dec 2, 2003 • Kellan

Paul Ford proves once again he is doing some of the most interesting work on the web. Compare Ftrain to your average blog. And check out his A Website for Harpers for a glimpse into an alternate reality where the web was realized as it was meant to be, and pets.com never existed.

All done up in that fin de siecle, “this website was typeset on a hand cranked printing press” signature Ftrain look. Spend a little while exploring, it is worth it.
